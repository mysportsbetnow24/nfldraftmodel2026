Draft Age Feature Added

What was added
- New loader: src/ingest/draft_age_loader.py
- New pull script: scripts/pull_espn_birthdates.py
- New templates:
  - data/sources/manual/espn_player_urls_2026_template.csv
  - data/sources/manual/espn_birthdates_2026_template.csv
- build_big_board now exports:
  - birth_date, draft_age, draft_age_source, draft_age_source_url, draft_age_ref_date, draft_age_available
  - formula_draft_age, formula_draft_age_bucket, formula_draft_age_context_adjustment, formula_draft_age_risk_adjustment

Draft date reference
- 2026-04-23 (can override with DRAFT_DATE env var)

Scoring behavior
- Draft-age scoring is OFF by default to avoid rewriting board behavior.
- Enable with: ENABLE_DRAFT_AGE_SCORING=1

How to populate from ESPN pages
1) Copy template to working input file:
   cp data/sources/manual/espn_player_urls_2026_template.csv data/sources/manual/espn_player_urls_2026.csv
2) Fill player URLs (and optional manual birth_date values).
3) Dry run:
   python3 scripts/pull_espn_birthdates.py --input data/sources/manual/espn_player_urls_2026.csv
4) Execute pull:
   python3 scripts/pull_espn_birthdates.py --input data/sources/manual/espn_player_urls_2026.csv --execute
5) Rebuild board:
   ALLOW_SINGLE_YEAR_PRODUCTION_KNN=1 python3 scripts/build_big_board.py

Notes
- qb_epa_per_pl alias support is active and maps to qb_epa_per_play.
- Non-position metrics are audited as ignored and do not affect unrelated positions.
