## Core Scouting Principle To Standardize
Evaluate prospects through the **entire rep**, not isolated flashes.

Rep sequence to log for every position:
1. Pre-snap alignment + leverage picture
2. First movement (release/get-off/drop)
3. Mid-rep processing and adjustment
4. Finish mechanics (ball placement/catch/finish/pursuit)
5. Outcome quality relative to expected leverage

Use this sequence in `How He Wins` and `Primary Concerns` so notes describe *how* traits show up, not just trait labels.

## QB Differentiation Framework (Film + Data)
### Film prompts
- Pocket rhythm and drop timing
- Field vision + anticipation windows
- Placement profile: underneath vs intermediate vs downfield
- Pressure response (reset, drift, panic, or controlled creation)
- Off-script value vs structure dependence

## WR/TE Differentiation Framework (Snap-to-Catch)
### Film prompts
- Acceleration off line to top speed
- Break mechanics: sink/gather/exit efficiency
- Edge attack and leverage manipulation
- Route precision and pacing
- Catch-point behavior: pluck/late hands/body control/contact finish
